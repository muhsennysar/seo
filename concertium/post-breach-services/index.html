What Are Post Breach Services?

Post breach services encompass a range of critical activities aimed at managing and mitigating the impact of a cyber breach. From initial incident response to long-term recovery, these services are essential for organizations looking to address breaches effectively while maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements. At Concertium, we offer a suite of post breach services that include Incident Response, Digital Forensics, Disclosure Communication Guidance, and Cyber Insurance Claim Preparedness.

How We Support Your Business

Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR)

When a breach occurs, rapid and thorough investigation is key. Concertium’s Digital Forensics and Incident Response team is equipped with advanced tools and methodologies to handle the complexities of breach management. Our experts lead the investigation to identify the root cause of the breach, contain and eradicate the threat, and restore normalcy to your operations.

First Responder's Incident Response Services

The initial moments following a breach are critical. Concertium’s First Responders are trained to engage immediately, triaging the situation and providing actionable guidance to manage the incident effectively.

Disclosure Communication Guidance

Clear and effective communication is vital during and after a breach. Our experts assist in developing communication strategies that address the needs of all stakeholders, including regulators, customers, and partners.

Cyber Insurance Claim Preparedness

Navigating the complexities of cyber insurance claims can be challenging, particularly after a breach. Concertium offers breach coaching to facilitate the claims process, ensuring that you have all necessary documentation and evidence to support your claim.

The Importance of Effective Post Breach Management

Managing a cyber breach effectively is crucial for several reasons. A poorly handled breach can lead to significant financial losses, reputational damage, regulatory fines, and legal liabilities. Concertium’s Post Breach Services are designed to address these challenges comprehensively, providing the necessary tools and support to manage breaches efficiently.
