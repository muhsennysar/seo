Our Services

Concertium’s Collective Coverage Suite (3CS)

The Collective Coverage Suite (3CS) is our flagship solution, integrating advanced cybersecurity services to deliver comprehensive protection against sophisticated threats. This suite includes real-time threat detection, automated network threat eradication, and post-breach management services, ensuring that your business is prepared to handle and recover from cyber incidents effectively.

1. Threat Detection and Response

Our Threat Precognition as a Service (TPaaS) leverages advanced technologies to provide early warning of potential attacks. This service includes:

  • Material Breach Monitoring & Alerting: Receive real-time alerts of active breaches, allowing you to take immediate action to stop the attack before it escalates.
  • AI-Enhanced Advanced Observability: Gain deep insights into your network, enabling you to identify anomalies and suspicious activities before they become serious threats.
  • Automated Threat Detection & Eradication: Our proprietary technology neutralizes threats within 30 seconds, ensuring minimal disruption to your operations.

2. Compliance and Risk Management

Navigating the complex regulatory landscape can be challenging, but with Concertium, you have a partner to guide you through it. Our services include:

  • Compliance Advisory Services: Stay ahead of regulatory requirements with ongoing guidance and support tailored to your specific industry needs.
  • Risk and Compliance Gap Analysis: Identify and address areas of non-compliance, helping you to build a robust security framework.
  • Baseline Infrastructure Security Review: Assess your current security posture and implement strategies to strengthen your defenses.

3. Managed Cybersecurity Services

Focus on your core business while we manage your cybersecurity needs. Our managed services include:

  • Managed Detection and Response (MDR): Continuous monitoring and threat response to detect and mitigate cyber threats in real-time.
  • Managed Network and Firewalls: Ensure the security of your network infrastructure with our proactive management and monitoring services.
  • Cloud Security: Protect your cloud environments from unauthorized access, data breaches, and other threats with our specialized security solutions.

4. Post-Breach Services

A cyber breach can have devastating consequences, but with Concertium, you are never alone. Our post-breach services are designed to help you recover quickly and minimize damage:

  • Digital Forensics and Incident Response: Our expert team will investigate the breach, contain the threat, and help you recover.
  • Cyber Insurance Claim Preparedness: We assist in preparing and submitting evidence to ensure your claim is processed smoothly.
  • Disclosure Communication Guidance: Effective communication is crucial in a crisis. We help you navigate disclosure requirements and manage stakeholder communication to protect your reputation.
